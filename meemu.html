MEEMU

A social platform that host's memes made by the best of us, for the rest of us.

Back To Projects

Meemu is a website targeted towards a wide audience, focusing on giving a place to find clean and top-tier memes to everyone, including the young.

As i've explored the internet, two things became very apparent.


1. There's a low chance I can show some of this stuff to my siblings...

2. In order to find the best, I had to trudge through the worst.

I hope that when this website is completed, it can give all a place to avoid the trudging, and find the gold!
